# Calculation of IGE and Social Mobility in South Korea and the US

This project analyzes intergenerational income elasticity (IGE) and social mobility in South Korea and the US. Three different panel data are used:

1. Korean Labor and Income Panel Study (KLIPS)
2. Korean Welfare Panel Study (KOWEPS)
3. Panel Study of Income Dynamics (PSID)

## Main goal
This project aims to (1) calculate the IGE, (2) compute social mobility.

## Data
Data are not shared due to confidentiality but can be downloaded at each websites:

1. KLIPS: https://www.kli.re.kr/klips
2. KOWEPS: https://www.koweps.re.kr/
3. PSID: https://psidonline.isr.umich.edu/
